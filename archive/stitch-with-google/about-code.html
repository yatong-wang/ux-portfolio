Get to know me

Integrity in coverage,
flexibility in writing.

My love affair with visual storytelling started very early; growing up in the bustling streets of Chicago did that to a person. As a kid, when there was no cable TV, you either played outside or read. I chose the latter, but soon found myself documenting the world around me.

Starting with a disposable camera and sketchbook, I ended up creating narratives from the mundane to the extraordinary, trying my hand at capturing the soul of a moment for the school paper.

Currently, I offer creative direction, photography, and brand strategy services to help my clients find their visual voice as seamlessly and painlessly as possible. I value authenticity above everything else, meaning I won't take 'good enough' as an answer.
